Ryan's Crystal Collector

Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.


There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.


Win the game by matching your total score to the randomized "Goal" number. You lose if your total score goes above the "Goal" number.


The value of each crystal is only revealed by clicking on it.


Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
